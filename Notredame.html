Our story is located in Paris in 1482. The situation of Paris at this time was sensitive. A lot of gipsys were living in Paris and they were not welcome. With the power of the Catholic Church against them having control of the government and the law. A bishop called Frollo started the hunting of the gipsys. "Clean the city of these heretics" . Fires and murders for all the region. But there was a little kid who recieved the mercy of Rollo.

Seventy years later the kid had grown safe and lonely in the cathedral of Notre Dame surronded of birds and gargoils as his friends He wasnt allowed to go out of the cathedral or down to the chapel. The only human who was allowed to talk was his sir and savior Rollo and his only rol was ring the bells and take care ot them.
