Bad Boys



A huge amount of drug was kept under the guard of the anti-drug department until some thieves stolen it by a tunnel. The plan of the thieves was simple. Make a deal to sell the drugs. Unfortunately one of the thieves fucked up and there was one witness who could destroy their plan.


A couple of police officers were assigned to find the drug and the thieves. With the witness under their protection they became from hunters to hunted.
